Album Redesign: NGHTMRE by NGHTMRE

Illustration & Typography

The album is about the Dream-like, invisible energy, trapped, hallucinating, healed mind, kind of feelings. The music itself has an EDM style. (The album’s name ‘Nghtmre’ constitute the same feeling as it’s music)

I wanted to connect those feelings with my album design. So I chose the fascinating mandala art to easily recognize the album genre. Mandala a Hinduism symbol of deeper connection with the self and the universe at large. Also people depict the mandala for enhancing focus, combatting stress and anxiety, appreciating the beauty of nature, and forming a greater connection with oneself. To reflect that connection, I decided to use mandala with ancient symbols like twelve planets, sacred sun, and moon sign, Hamsa, and flowers. On the back panel, the artist’s photo with moon sign emphasis the mandala and the fading off a song list incorporating the effects of ‘Mandala’.
