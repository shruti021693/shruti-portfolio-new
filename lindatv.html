Linda Microsoft Smart TV

UI/UX Design and Motion Graphics

Microsoft is coming out with voice assistant smart TV. It allows you not only to watch movies but also goes beyond entertainment and let work as a virtual assistant by interacting with multiple devices on a network with a personable and intuitive interface. Linda follows the Microsoft interface and designs. It provides novel features like hand free assistant tasks, controls smart home devices, individual voice recognition, accent and slang comprehension, complex command, parental control and so more.

Linda learns through their network of established users how to provide the richest experience, right out of the box. Moreover, reduces the complexity of multiple services and subscription and provides a standard, trustworthy individual interface.

Onboarding Video

For onboarding, Linda tries to make the process short and get essential data through connected resources. It learns through the user's voice and tone and begins to communicate. Linda prefers that users should use their sensitive data like passwords, payment methods in a mobile app to diminish the chances of misuse.

Video

Linda goes beyond entertainment and visual communication. Users can play recipe videos and share the same video link immediately to friends with multiple social apps with the help of Linda Smart TV. For parental control, Linda can protect children from harmful content and set a time limit. Moreover, It can understand your voice tone and slang and respond to you in an appropriate way.
