Logo & Rebranding

This is a self-made side project for the beloved brand. Rebranding the Starbucks logo in a way that combing the original logo and updated logo. This is a challenging project to touch the prestigious design and work on itâ€™s futuristic and intricate details.

In the first logo the two-tailed mermaid associated with nature, and stability. Later, the siren logo perpetually turned into a kelly green and white palette to enhance a fresh start, growth, and prosperity. And the new logo recognizes for a minimalist, apparently symmetric, textless, and natural shapes to embraces and respects our heritage and at the same time.

The redesigned logo shows the old two-tailed mermaid with the new logo mold. For keeping the consistency of minimalism, a strong element of freedom, organic vibes, eye-friendly aspects of the logo, I mainly focused on the mermaid. Her face, crown, and caudal fins are mainly from the original logo and radically modified her eyes, nose, and lip. I manipulated the hairlines in respect of her face angle edges to be compatible with the new logo. The scales on the tails are a bit different so it expresses the original logo appearance. Her torso and hand remain alike as the new logo. I did not touch the color palette and try to maintain the original Starbucks green, Accent Green, Cool natural White, and White.
