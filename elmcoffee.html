Elm Coffee Roasters

Packaging, Illustration and Visual Design

TElm Coffee Roasters currently offers only bagged products (grounds and beans). With more and more people on the go and higher out-of-home coffee consumption, Elm Coffee Roasters is looking to expand and connect with consumers in the on-the-go context by launching a single-serve RTD iced coffee offering.

For the school project, I have to design the packaging for Elm Coffee Roastersâ€™ RTD coffee can. The brand is originally sophisticated, high scale, and works with premium and organic products. The challenge was the visual identity should translate the brand into producing RTD coffee packaging with excellence, harmony, and to generate satisfaction and well-being to its customers, offering a clean organic environment feel.

Elm Coffee Roasters is connected to the Ethiopian coffee roots and depicting their colors from freshly brewed coffee, raw green beans, delicate creamy hull, and aromatic brown. For representing natural feel I drew a free hand of a coffee stem sketch showing values of the brand. For keeping the packaging clean and minimal I mainly focused on typography and positioning. The overall design follows the brand identity and culture.
